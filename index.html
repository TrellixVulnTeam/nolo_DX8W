Austin, TX-based emo-punks nolo set out to write something snappy… “Deals” came out of that writing session – and even just ten seconds of listening to its blistering pace is enough to know they hit their mark. The quartet each took a turn at the microphone in this song as they explore themes of self-toxicity and their insecurities, the perfect lyrical subject for an angsty indie-emo band like this. Fans of Oso Oso, Prince Daddy & The Hyena, and Microwave are going to like this one a lot.
